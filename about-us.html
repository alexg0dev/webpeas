About WebPeas.live

Our company, our story, and our commitment to providing affordable website solutions.

Our Company

WebPeas.live was created by Dev Anonymous, who serves as the CEO and lead developer of our organization. Founded in 2025, our service operates under the umbrella of two established entities: ag0dev development and Melissa's Melts LTD.

We are legally registered and based in the United Kingdom, adhering to all applicable UK business regulations and data protection laws. Our business model focuses on providing affordable, high-quality website development services to small businesses and entrepreneurs.

Our mission is to democratize web presence by making professional websites accessible to everyone, regardless of budget constraints. We believe that every business deserves a strong online identity without the traditional high costs associated with web development.

What We Do

We provide affordable, professional website development services with a focus on quality and accessibility.

Static Website Development

We specialize in creating custom static websites using HTML, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript. Our standard package includes up to 4 pages with responsive design, ensuring your site looks great on all devices.

Custom Add-Ons

For businesses requiring more advanced functionality, we offer custom add-ons including booking systems, payment integrations, admin dashboards, databases, and advanced forms. Each add-on is priced transparently.

Free Hosting

We provide free hosting for all websites we develop. Clients are only responsible for purchasing their domain name. Our hosting is reliable and secure, ensuring your website remains accessible to your customers.
